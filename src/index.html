CASE DESCRIPTION

  • 70,000 people from 10 to 85 years old take photos of themselves and turn their heads to the smartphone camera
  • The dataset consists of selfies using the front camera of the different smartphones, including iPhone, Xiaomi, Samsung, OPPO, Honor, Vivo
  • The dataset includes video on the front camera of a smartphone, in which a person turns his head in different directions
download sample

Technial specification

  • Each set includes person's gender, age, ethnicity, video resolution, duration and frames per second
  • The video durations varies from 10 seconds to 30 seconds, depends on the person
  • Mostly inhouse with diverse background and various lighting
  • Agreement when providing data for each person
  • The dataset meets the requirements of ISO 30107-3 for Bona Fide Presentation
  • Data allows you to reduce Bona Fide Presentation Classificaiton Error Rate
